Horton Hears a Boom

We all know the classic Dr. Sues story, but what if Horton was an office worker and the mysterious noise was a bomb? See the chaos that ensues while Horton and his boss try to deal with this outrageous situation. Will Horton’s keen ears help save the day? This video was the final production of my intro to electronic media production class. For the production I was the screenwriter and director.

CREATED SEP 20, 2020

Dirtballs Forever!

Witness the rise of a competitive spikeball team known as the Dirtballs. Their elite level of play has led them to the championship game. With the title on the line their skills are put to the test against their biggest rivals. They will soon find out that winning won't be easy and there will be numerous obstacles to overcome. This emotional journey has everything a sports fan is looking for and more.

Lundendary's Survival Anthology

Join Professor Julius Lundendary and Billy on an educational journey about the wonders of the outdoors. Learn about the essentials of survival and do your best to follow along with Billy as Lundendary exercises his extensive knowledge and expertise. You'll be suprised how easy it is to find a salami sandwhich in the woods.

CREATED OCT 12, 2020

End of the World

The craziest thing to ever happen in history caught on camera, but you can’t see it. This parody video features a vlogger who is more focused on filming his own reaction than what is happening in front of him. Your only clue to what is going on are the sounds of the event. This video was created for my moving images class and was an exercise in audio mixing and using sound to help tell the story.

Skateboards and Burritos

Nothing is better on a rainy day than a burrito. And there is only one way to get there. Four wheels mounted to a piece of wood. However, as the saying goes the journey is more important than the destination. Even if the destination involves a tortilla filled with meat and cheese. Come take a tour through familiar locations in Minneapolis and the Twin-Cities campus in the rain.
